CCJPA Bulk CMS Schedule Generator

How to update the website

Upload your 3 GTFS files (stops.txt, trips.txt, stop_times.txt). This tool will automatically process all 4 schedule variations (Weekday/Weekend, Eastbound/Westbound) and combine them into a single, tabbed HTML block formatted specifically for WordPress. Click "Copy HTML Code" and paste it into the HTML/Code editor on your page.
